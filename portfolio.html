Portfolio

A bit of what I did on the places I've been

UFRGS Mobile

iOS • Android

UFRGS Mobile is UFRGS's official mobile app. With it, students can see news about the university, check the status of loaned books and access the daily menu of the university restaurant. It is available both for iOS and Android.

My contribution

Me and my teammates redesigned the whole application, aiming to improve user experience and optimize its performance in both systems: iOS and Android.

Brique UFRGS

iOS

A mobile application for the several departments of UFRGS to be able to exchange assets not being used, such as furniture and gadgets.

My contribution

I took part in the whole development proccess of the iOS version, from the layout decisions to the final implementation.

UFRGS Mapas

iOS • Android

This application helps UFRGS's students and workers to find the location and more information about all of the university's buildings. It's especially helpful for newcomers and visitants.

My contribution

My part in this project was to reformulate its layout and correct some outdated data bugs.

Player Sample

iOS • Android

On my internship at globo.com, I developed a functional application as a proof of concept to the mobile video player (the main product of my development team back then) used on globo.com's products.

My contribution

I developed the application (for both platfoms, iOS and Android) following a pre-determined layout and using a compilation of videos of several types, to test all the functionalities available on the video player.
