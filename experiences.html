Experiences

Places where I contributed and learnt a lot of cool stuff

Apple Developer Academy

february/2015 - december/2015

Apple Developer Academy (formerly known as BEPiD) is a program that aims to empower IT students to build iOS apps, from the research envolved in the idea maturarion, going through development itself and culminating in the proccess of publishing the app to App Store and promoting it.

This was my first contact with the iOS plataform as a developer, and it really hooked me.

globo.com

june/2016 - november/2017

I started my internship at globo.com in mid 2016, working in the team responsible for the video player component (mobile version), which would later be used in apps such as Globoplay and Globosat Play. Mostly, I worked with Swift, Java and Kotlin.

Afterwards, I rotated to the team responsible for the the main content publishing platform in globo.com. In this project, I worked with Python, in the context of backend web (mainly tornado framework). Back in the day, I also had some experience with Javascript in the frontend.

CPD - UFRGS

august/2018 - november/2019

I started working as an iOS developer in UFRGS Data Center (Centro de Processamento de Dados) by August 2018. I had the oportunity to continue learning about iOS development, expanded my experiences in Android, and had several learnings related to UI and UX. Besides, I had a brief experience with Wordpress.

Poatek

november/2019 - may/2020

By November 2019, I took a next step in my Software Developer career: I started working full-time as an iOS engineer in Poatek. There, I developed my skills in iOS development, by learning about software architecture, patterns and good practices.

Also, at Poatek, I got more experienced with Agile methods and was able to have more contact with the customer of the project I was working on, which allowed me to develop my interpersonal skills.
